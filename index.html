About Web3Bridge

Web3bridge is a program created in 2019 to train Web3 developers in Africa. They are working on building sustainable Web3 economy in Africa through remote and onsite Web3 development training, supporting web3 developers and startups, and lowering barriers of entry into the Web3 ecosystem.
